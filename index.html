About the Institute

A Legacy of Shaping Educators

Founded by the Ratlam Maa Sharda Uchcha Shiksha Samiti, SSIT stands as a centre of excellence for teacher education in Madhya Pradesh — nurturing competent, compassionate educators since 2000.

  • Located near Ratlam Railway Station, with excellent connectivity
  • Spacious, modern campus within Ratlam Municipal Corporation limits
  • Fully equipped classrooms, labs, and library
  • Affiliated with the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE)
  • Dedicated faculty with deep academic expertise

Academic Programmes

Pathways to the Teaching Profession

01

B.Ed — Bachelor of Education

A professional undergraduate degree for graduates aspiring to teach at secondary and senior secondary levels. Emphasises pedagogy, subject mastery, and classroom excellence.

2 Years · Full Time
02

D.El.Ed — Diploma in Elementary Education

A nationally recognised diploma equipping candidates to teach at the primary and upper-primary level. Aligned with NCTE norms and RTE mandates.

2 Years · Full Time
03

Alumni & Continuing Education

SSIT's alumni network offers lifelong learning, professional workshops, and career support for graduates in the education sector across Madhya Pradesh.
